OUR SUCCESS STORY

When I was a child, I didn’t do well at school, I wasn’t good at things like maths and physics. I was fond of drawing cars engines. Since my father was a mechanic, I decided to leave school and work with him in the garage. Days after days I learned the job and became skilled in repairing cars.

I got married soon after and had a family. My eldest son who was brilliant at school , was fond of cars as well. He used to spend his free time in the garage trying to fix things and helping me doing things in a better way.

Day by day , my son got his diploma and became a mechanical engineer. He was motivated to invent new strategies and create new approaches to foster our work and make of it a great success. Nowadays, we bought another garage with new and high technology , also we created this website which made of our team very successful and outstanding all over the country.
